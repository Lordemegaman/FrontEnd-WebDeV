About the Caribbean Islands

  • The Greater Antilles
  • Bahamas
  • Lesser Antilles

The Greater Antilles is a group of large islands in the western Caribbean, including Cuba, Hispaniola (shared by Haiti and the Dominican Republic), Jamaica, and Puerto Rico. These islands boast a wealth of cultural heritage, stunning landscapes, and vibrant communities.
