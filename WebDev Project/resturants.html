Top 3 Places to Eat

Beachside at The Sands resort on Providenciales, Hemingway's has ranked as a favorite place for visitors and locals to gather since 1998. In addition to elevating island-style specialties like conch empanada and coconut shrimp with mango chili goat cheese curd sauce, the restaurant has built a reputation to match. It's open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Perched atop a sunset-facing bluff, Sheer Rocks specializes in tapas, cocktails, and an a la carte menu featuring dishes made from locally sourced ingredients whenever possible. Everything is handmade in house, with all fish being line-caught and all lobster hand-dived.

On the date night scene, this romantic, adults-only enclave stands out for its exclusive feel, serene seaside setting, and lean towards healthy eating. Part of Bucuti & Tara Beach Resort, its contemporary indoor-outdoor flow fits with its clean, thoughtfully crafted cuisine and cocktails.
